When did Pizza appear in the India ?

Pizza was first introduced to India in the early 1900s by Italian missionaries. During British rule, it came back to the limelight for a while. At first, it was not very popular, but it slowly began to gain in popularity over time. In the 1970s, there was major growth in the popularity of pizza. (Source: The Delicious Story of How Pizza Evolved in India)
